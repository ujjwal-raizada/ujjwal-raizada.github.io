About the Book

This Book will help you in everyday lives, and in every relationship. You will also be able to differentiate between Illusion, Mystery and Practical world and how you can live better in all of them. To explain this, I have chosen “Adam and Eve” as they were the first human beings of the world. This book will be helpful to everyone, regardless of religion, caste, age or gender.

  • This book also explains how the world began, how it became like the present and what the reasons behind it are, how you can change your world, and live a better life in the present world in all aspects.
  • Buddhas love us with overflowing love. Do you know why we killed them?
  • There is no other option now, to save every individual without following the Buddha’s suggestions.
  • Meditation is the only way to live happily, there is no other way.

About the Author




Saurabh Srivastava works for a reputed online e-commerce company. He was born in Bihar (India) and grew up in Lucknow (India). He works on self-improvement. In his spare time, he reads mythological and spiritual books, listens to classical music and writes. He lives in Delhi at present.
